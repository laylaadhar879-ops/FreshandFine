Our Story

Fresh & Fine was founded in 1995 with nothing but a pattion for concucting homemade fruit juice and a love for making the community happy. Our fathers and grandfathers started the farm from scratch, working tirelessly to cultivate the land and grow the best fruits possible, along with many hours of tial to perfect the recipe.Our commitment to quality and sustainability drives everything we do

Over the years, we have expanded our product line to include a variety of fresh products such as eggs, milk sourced straight from our cows and ice-creams all made with the same care and attention to detail that our founders instilled in us. We are proud to be a family-owned business that values tradition, community, and the environment.

Our Family

As a family we have worked hard over many years and generations to build a sustainable and thriving farm. After many trials and tribulations that our forefathers have faced, we have succeeded in creating a legacy of quality and care while still being a loving community.

Our Animals

The animals on the farm are treated with love and care to ensure their well-being and happiness. They are fed with the best quality grains and food to keep them healthy and strong.

Our Farm

Our farm spans over several acres of fertile land where we cultivate a variety of fruits and vegetables without the use of any harmful chemicals, we use sustainable farming practices which requires more time and effort but allows us to protect the environment and produce high-quality organic products.

Our Produce

We take great pride in the fresh fruits and vegetables we grow on our farm. Our produce is carefully looked after everyday and harvested at the peak of ripeness to ensure the best flavor and nutritional value for our customers.
